About Desiree

Therapeutic care that
meets you where you are

I'm Desiree Dekle, a Licensed Massage Therapist based in Lakeland, Florida. My approach combines evidence-based techniques with an understanding of how your body actually works — not just how it feels in the moment.

Whether you're recovering from an injury, managing chronic pain, or simply need to unwind, I build each session around your specific goals. I work with athletes, veterans, and everyday people who are ready to feel better and move more freely.

As an approved VA Community Care Network provider, I'm proud to serve the men and women who've served our country.
